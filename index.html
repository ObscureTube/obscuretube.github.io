About ObscureTube


This website displays recent, low view count YouTube videos with names like IMG 1234, MOV 9876, or DSC 5678.

This site is similar others that also display low view count videos, such as astronaut.io or petittube.com, but this site aims to add some functionality that I felt was missing from those. Using astronaut.io, I quickly found myself looping and watching the same videos again, and the feed was overrun by a handful of channels that were posting dozens or hundreds of times a day.

Here, every video ID you see is cached in your cookies, so you should never see the same video twice. You also have the option to block channels from showing up entirely if you get tired of them. No data about your session is stored server side, and your experience can be refreshed by clearing your cookies or resetting your cache.

There's other features, such as customizable autoskip & options to share the site. More to come.
