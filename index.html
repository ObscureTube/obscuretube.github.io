About ObscureTube



ObscureTube displays recent, low view count YouTube videos with random names like IMG 1234, MOV 9876, or DSC 5678.

ObscureTube is similar to other sites like astronaut.io or petittube.com, but adds some new features. To avoid repition, ObscureTube videos watched are cached in your cookies & you can also optionally block channels entirely. Refresh your feed by clearing your cookies or resetting the cache. Additional features include customizable autoskip & sharing options. More coming soon.
